CfA Solar Physics REU!

Advisors: Dr. Kamen Kozarev and Dr. Kelly Korreck

For my project, I catalogued coronal waves to learn more about the relationship between shock waves in the solar corona and solar energetic particles (SEPs).

Some – but not all – shock waves eject large fluxes of SEPs that can instigate bad space weather. We want to find out which types of shocks unleash these events.

             

SDO video of a 04/23/13 shock wave. Where is it?             After image processing, here it is!                            

To study this phenomenon, I identified candidate shock wave events from here.
I then wrote an IDL program to measure these waves' kinematics, as shown below.
This kinematic data helped confirm the majority of these wave events to be shocks.

            

The yellow curve shows the shock wave's distance from the Sun over time as it rises through the corona.
