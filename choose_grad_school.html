Graduate School

If you participated in research as an undergraduate and really enjoyed it, grad school is definitely worth considering.

At some point, you will have to decide whether to grad school.
I plan to have some links on that in the future.

If you do decide to go to graduate school, you will have to apply to a whole bunch of different places.
Once you are admitted, most places will provide you with the opportunity to visit to get a feel for what the department and to meet your potential advisors. Finding an advisor that is a good research fit and someone you would like to work with is one of the most important things to look for when choosing which grad school to attend.
