CS 6780!

I was a teaching assistant for CS 6780, a graduate-level introduction to machine learning taught by Thorsten Joachims. My responsbilities involved grading homework assignments and an exam.

The graduate level course is nearly identical to the undergraduate version of the course, except that it is taught at a faster pace and has a more demanding final project.
The undergraduate version is available online with lecture slides and videos, but not assignments or exams.
