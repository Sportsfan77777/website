Research!

I study the formation and evolution of planets and other objects in planetary systems.

At Steward Observatory, I am working with Professor Kaitlin Kratter.

While I was an undergraduate at Cornell, I primarily worked in two research groups led by Professors Dong Lai and Gordon Stacey.
I also participated in two summer programs where I became a member of both the CASTLE group led by Professor Simon Portegies Zwart and the SAO Solar Physics group where I worked with Dr. Kamen Kozarev.
