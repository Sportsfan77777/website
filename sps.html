Society of Physics Students! (SPS!)

            

Cornell SPS members run various demos at EYH from a bunch of different reference frames.                

I became the outreach coordinator of Cornell's SPS chapter because I wanted to get other students involved in outreach activities.

My favorite events were the Expanding Your Horizons (EYH) conferences. Each year, SPS members would run a demo booth (see photo above) that EYH attendees could check out during breaks between workshops.
In my junior year, we also created a simple take-home workshop in which we gave out CDs that attendees could use to make into spectrometers.
In my senior year, my successor to the outrech coordinator position, Omar, led a workshop in which attendees made their own radios!

We also participated in NanoDays (see photo below), among other local events.

            

A Cornell SPS member co-hosts a booth for NanoDays at the Ithaca Sciencenter.
