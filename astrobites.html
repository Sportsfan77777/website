Astrobites!

            

The Astrobites logo                

I write for Astrobites>, a graduate student-run website dedicated to making astronomy publications more accesible to undergraduates.

I read Astrobites throughout my four years as an undergraduate, and felt it helped me realize that I did want to become an astronomer. It also helped me learn about opportunities in astronomy (for undergraduates, and also further down the road), and become accustomed to modern areas of research (which is useful for figuring out what sub-field of astronomy to pursue [exoplanets, for me!], and for understanding colloquia).
Given the role it has played in my life, I highly recommend reading Astrobites to anyone considering a career in astronomy.
