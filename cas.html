Cornell Astronomical Society! (CAS!)

            

The CAS crew. Our 12-inch refractor Irv is in the background.                

I am a proud almunus and former president of the Cornell Astronomical Society, Cornell's amateur astronomy club. Cornell offers a unique opportunity to not only have the on-campus Fuertes Observatory, but also to have weekly public stargazing nights that are primarily run by the student members of CAS!!! :-)

Check out the CAS Website for the most up-to-date info about the club.
The history of Fuertes Observatory in particular makes a great story.
